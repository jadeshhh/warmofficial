Meet the Creators

Jade Shen is a Bronx Science Junior.
From a robotics programmer to an ice skater fanatic, Jade Shen has always strived to break the limits placed on her. As a young female in STEM, she plans to put forth only her best work!

"Warm" was a game she and two others decided to create to promote mental health. As an adolscent female, she has seen the effects mental health could have on people firsthand. She believes that by creating this game, she will be able to spread more awareness about mental health issues, and teach others that their happiness matters.
